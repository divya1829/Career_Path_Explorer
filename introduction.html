Welcome to Career Path Explorer

Career Path Explorer is a free and simple platform designed to guide students in choosing the right career after their current stage of education — whether they are in school, college, or just finished their B.Tech.

Our goal is to provide:

  • Clear career options after 10th, Intermediate, and B.Tech
  • Information on skill-based and degree-based paths
  • Guidance for competitive exams and entrance tests
  • Insights into various career fields like coding, designing, government jobs, etc.

Whether you're confused or curious, Career Path Explorer is here to help you take the next step confidently.
