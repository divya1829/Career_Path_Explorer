Career Paths After 12th

Discover your options after 12th standard across Science, Commerce, Arts, and Skill-based courses to build a successful future.

Science Stream

  • Engineering: Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical
  • Medical: MBBS, BDS, Pharmacy, Nursing
  • Pure Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics
  • IT & Software: BCA, Cybersecurity, Software Development
  • Architecture & Design: B.Arch, Interior Design

Commerce Stream

  • Accounting & Finance: B.Com, Chartered Accountancy (CA)
  • Business Management: BBA, MBA (Post Graduation)
  • Economics & Banking: Economics Degree, Banking Jobs
  • Insurance & Taxation: Insurance Sector, Tax Consultancy

Arts / Humanities Stream

  • Social Sciences: History, Political Science, Psychology
  • Languages & Literature: Journalism, Mass Communication
  • Design & Fine Arts: Graphic Design, Fashion, Animation
  • Law: BA LLB (Integrated Law Course)
